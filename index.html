Our Story

Built on faith, driven by purpose

Founded in 2026, Iglesia ni Chancellor was born out of a simple vision: to create a place where people from all walks of life can encounter God's love, grow in their faith, and serve their community. We are a church that believes in the transforming power of the Gospel, the importance of genuine fellowship, and the call to make a difference in the world around us.

Faith

Grounded in Scripture and the timeless truth of God's Word, we seek to deepen our relationship with Christ every day.

Community

We believe that life is better together. Our doors are open to everyone seeking genuine connection and belonging.

Service

Called to serve, we extend God's love beyond our walls through outreach, compassion, and acts of kindness.

Our Benefits

Exclusive benefits of being part of our community

No Monthly Dues

Membership is entirely free. We focus on spiritual growth and fellowship, without any hidden charges or mandatory fees.

Unli Dinuguan sa Kapilya

Enjoy unlimited traditional Dinuguan every time we gather. A delicious, warm meal shared together in unity.

Walang Kaso

A peaceful community with clean slates. We resolve conflicts through grace, forgiveness, and positive communication.

Anti Kurap

Absolute transparency and integrity. Every resource is accounted for and directed entirely to supporting our church family.
